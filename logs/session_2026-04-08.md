10. **Re-derived the plane-induced homography from scratch.** With
    `X_dst = R_ds X_src + t_ds` and the plane in src as `n_s^T X_s = d_s`,
    for X_s on the plane:

        X_d = R_ds X_s + t_ds (n_s^T X_s / d_s) = (R_ds + t_ds n_s^T / d_s) X_s

    i.e. **plus**, not the minus I had copied from a textbook reference.
    The minus form is for the opposite (src, dst) ordering / inward normal.
    Logged as **R-004** in `plan.md`. Fixed `plane_homography` and re-ran:

    | episode | raw disp | stab disp | reduction | inlier H-RMSE |
    |---------|---------:|----------:|----------:|--------------:|
    |       0 |     6.05 |      0.92 |      6.6× |          1.49 |
    |       1 |     8.98 |      2.11 |     4.25× |          5.45 |

    These are honest stabilization wins. Run log:
    `logs/runs/2026-04-08_*_egostabilizer_basic_pick_place_1_signfix.log`.

11. **Updated H-RMSE to be inlier-filtered** (median of best 50% per pair).
    Without filtering the metric is dominated by ORB matches on the moving
    hand and the manipulated object — content that no plane homography can
    explain. With filtering, single-digit pixels means the planar background
    is correctly tracked. Updated the metric definition section in
    `doc.md` §8.5.2 to call this out.
